How to create Safe Transaction?

To initiate a "Safe Transaction" you must connect your wallet with NFT, or the amount you are willing to trade for. Do this by clicking on the "Connect Wallet" button above, choose the wallet which will be used to trade through Safe Transaction feature. You have successfully logged into your wallet, choose the NFT and amount you are trading for.

As soon as you request a Safe Transaction, a notification about the active one will be sent to the wallet of the person with whom you are making the trade
